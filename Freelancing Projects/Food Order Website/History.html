We cook the best food in the entire city, with excellent customer service, the best meals and at the best price, visit us.The term “bistro” is believed to have originated from the Russian word “bystro,” which means “quickly. ” According to a popular legend, this term was used by Russian soldiers during the occupation of Paris in 1815 when they wanted food and drink. A more credible etymology traces the name to “bistrouille,” a northern French term for a mixture of coffee and eau-de-vie, which was a common order in 19th-century Parisian bistros.

Bistros emerged as small, neighborhood restaurants in Paris during the 19th century. They were known for serving simple, hearty meals and offering a casual, convivial atmosphere. Over time, the concept of the bistro spread to other parts of the world, including the United States, where it has evolved into a variety of different styles and formats.
