How to Reach

Located in the heart of city and only 8 kms from the airport, The K Tree Hotel, is about 15 minute drive from the airport and about 5 minute drive from the Chatrapati Shahu Maharaj Kolhapur Railway station. The hotel is just 2KM away from Pune – Bangalore NH4 national highway which provides excellent connectivity to all the commuters via Kolhapur.

By Air


Kolhapur airport has direct flights from Mumbai. Hotel K Tree is just 9 KM away from the airport which is approximately 15 minute drive to the Hotel.

By Rail


Shri Chatrapati Shahu Maharaj Terminus is very well connected and has 3 daily services to and fro from Mumbai. It is also well connected to Southern & other parts of the country. Hotel K Tree is just 1.5 kms away from the railway station – which is just a 5 minute drive.


By Road


This Hotel is just 1 kms away from the central bus stand and also just 2 kms away from NH4 (Pune – Bangalore) highway which provides excellent connectivity to all visitors to Kolhapur and also as stop over for by-passers on the highway.


Direction


Travel from Airport & Railway Station & Bus Stand to Hotel
